The golden sections with the light yellow lines look like scratches on brass instruments. The tiny black lines within the frame of the blue centered square could be transcribed musical notes. Although there’s a subtle fluidity to this piece, he creates contrast by combining the organic elements of the scribbles with the hard, structured corners of the large geometric shapes. He’s created controlled chaos.
